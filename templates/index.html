Ask any experienced web developer and they'll tell you that no two e-commerce projects are alike - each requires unique customisations tailored to the product and business at hand. There are some huge shopping cart applications available that include every feature you'd ever need and while these get you most of the way there - in reality trying to wedge that last bit of customisation into a monolithic design can turn out to be a real project killer.

Cartridge is an incredibly light-weight shopping cart framework that brings together the minimum required feature set to get you up and running with a compact and flat structure using nothing but standard Django idioms. With this simplistic approach, adding on your own tailored functionality is a breeze.

Features

  • Hierarchical categories
  • Easily configurable product options (colours, sizes, etc.)
  • Hooks for shipping calculations and payment gateway
  • Sale pricing
  • Stock management
  • Product popularity
  • Thumbnail generation
  • Complete unit test coverage
  • Total separation of presentation (no embedded markup)
  • Denormalised data built for accessiblilty and performance
